1950s Bungalow

The 1950s bungalow is a two story home located in Bountiful, UT. The intent of this design is to conserve its inviting and cozy feel while creating a modern and functional environment by bringing a complete furniture refresh. With the implementation of more light and storage Mr. Miller will be able to sell the house faster to an ideal family.
