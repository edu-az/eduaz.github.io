Overview

Purpose

I am a Computer Information Technology student at BYU-I. I love technology and I am passionate about helping people grow their personal brand or business. I build easy-to-use websites tailored to their needs, thinking about the user and target audience at the same time. I believe in the power of collaboration and brainstorming to get a good picture of the desired product. Because I value communication, I am open to input and feedback on my work. I want to meet the expectations of my clients to make them happy.

Audience

The target audience is interior design professional companies that focus on both commercial and residential design. These companies have interior design professionals looking to recruit interns as well as find new employees to join their team. They will be able to get to know the individual as well as their work and experience in interior design. The website portfolio also allows them to directly contact the individual through social media as well as their personal email.
