Overview

Purpose

We want to bring people together by helping them discover new rafting spots in Idaho. We want to build a community where people can share their experiences and advice for whitewater rafting. Our hope is that our platform can bring people together to have fun and relax. We provide valuable information about different whitewater rafting locations, weather, level of difficulty and information for renting kayaks and rafts. There is plenty of information online, but we feel that there needs to be a way to share and read their own adventures. We are set aside from other companies because we are built by the community for the community.

Audience

My target audience is people from 20-40 years old who are enthusiastic about whitewater rafting. These people are millennials and Gen Z looking to get out of their comfort zone to embark on new adventures. They need to escape stress from their day-to-day life, so we offer them a platform with information on how to do that. People from college students to managers or anyone who would like to experience both calm waters as well as the rapids. They want to have an immemorable experience that makes them want to come back each year and bring new friends. Our platform allows them a place to share their experiences.
